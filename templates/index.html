How YouTube Earnings Work

CPM stands for Cost Per Mille — how much advertisers pay per 1,000 views. It varies by:

  • Niche: Finance ($5-$15) vs Gaming ($1-$3)
  • Audience: US/UK viewers pay more
  • Content: Advertiser-friendly videos earn more

Formula: Earnings = (Views × Monetized %) × (CPM ÷ 1000)
